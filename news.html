What is the future of dominos? Domino's Pizza, Inc. unveiled ambitious growth goals during its 2019 Investor Relations Day on Thursday. The Ann Arbor, Mich. -based pizza chain — which currently includes 15,300 stores globally — expects to grow by nearly 60 percent over the next six years, with a target goal of 9,700 new stores by 2025.
